Rice Recipe

Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly.
  2. Place the rice and water in a saucepan.
  3. Add salt and bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 18-20 minutes or until the rice is tender.
  5. Fluff with a fork and serve.
